Lakbai
Lakbai is a travel website created as part of a group project for an academic requirement. I am also the project's lead programmer, and the website was developed collaboratively. This website displays tourist locations in the Philippines, as well as an estimate of the entire cost of travel and dining costs. It also displays the geolocation of the tourist attraction so that visitors may easily reach it. The website's functionality is CRUD (create, read, update, and delete), and there are three user levels: the administrator, who supervises tourist places and lower user levels; the user, who can create reviews for tours; and the non-user, who may browse the website but with limited functionality.
